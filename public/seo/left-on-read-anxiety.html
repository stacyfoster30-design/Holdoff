The Neuroscience of the Read Receipt

When you see the "Read" notification without a reply, your brain registers it as an active rejection rather than a passive delay. For those with anxious attachment, the silence is deafening, and the impulse to double-text is an attempt to self-soothe.

What to do instead

The most effective interruption is physical. Put your phone down. The message will still be there in an hour, and you will be better equipped to see it clearly.
