# HoldOff — Google Play Store Listing

## App Name
HoldOff — Build Your Pause

## Short Description (80 chars max)
AI-powered dialer & SMS app that helps you pause before you send.

## Full Description (4000 chars max)

**HoldOff is your default phone and messaging app — with an AI co-pilot that helps you communicate from your best self.**

Every text you're about to send, every call you're about to make — HoldOff gives you a moment to pause. Not to stop you. To help you choose.

---

### 🧠 The HoldOff Filter
Type your message. Before you hit send, run it through the Filter. In seconds, you get:
- **SEND / WAIT / DO NOT SEND** — a clear verdict
- The emotional pattern behind your message (anxious protest, love bombing, stonewalling, etc.)
- A reframe of what you're actually feeling
- A better version of your message if you still want to reach out

### 📱 Full Dialer & SMS
HoldOff is a complete, fully-featured phone and messaging app:
- Keypad, call history, contacts
- SMS inbox, conversation threads, group messaging
- All the features you expect — plus the ones that protect you

### 🤖 AI Companions
Four AI companions, each with a distinct attachment style, to help you process what's happening:
- **Sadie** — warm, secure-leaning guide
- **Stacy** — raw, fearful-avoidant honesty
- **Danny** — calm, dismissive-avoidant logic
- **Dan** — direct, energetic hype coach

### 🔒 Private by Default
HoldOff uses **on-device AI (Gemini Nano)** by default. Your messages never leave your phone. No data sent to servers. No exceptions.

### ♿ Accessibility First
- Adjustable text size (85%–150%)
- High contrast mode
- Full TalkBack / screen reader support
- Haptic feedback
- Reduce motion option

### 🌀 Spiral Lock
Gotten three DO NOT SEND verdicts in a row? HoldOff recognizes the spiral and locks the filter for a cooling-off period — protecting you from yourself in the moments that matter most.

---

**HoldOff is not a therapist. It's the pause you never had.**

If you're in crisis, please call or text 988.

## Data Safety
- No data collected by default (on-device AI)

- No data sold to third parties
- No data used for advertising
