# HoldOff Wiki

## Our Story
HoldOff was built from personal necessity—a tool created because it was needed, and then shared with the world in the certainty that others struggling with the same emotions needed it, too. It is designed to help you build your pause.

## Core Features
- **The Pause:** A space to step back when emotions spiral.
- **Sadie:** Your AI companion, grounded in empathy and the story of HoldOff's creation.
- **Personalized Insights:** Pattern tracking and feedback tailored to your needs.
- **Mental Health Focus:** A safe environment for managing RSD, anxiety, depression, addiction, attachment styles, and more. *Note: HoldOff is not therapy, diagnosis, or a substitute for professional care.*

## Navigation
- **Home:** Your landing point for the pause.
- **Inbox:** Your messages and threads.
- **Contacts:** Sync and manage the people who matter most.
- **Settings:** Customize your experience, edit launch-condition categories, and view your personal insights.

## Our Mission
To provide a transformational and desirable experience for the people we love who also struggle. HoldOff is about connection, understanding, and the power of a simple pause.
