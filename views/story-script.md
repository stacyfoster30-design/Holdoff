# HoldOff Premium Story Script

## Scene 1: The Moment (Stacy's Real Story)

### Stacy's Narration (Voice: Thoughtful, vulnerable, authentic)
"This is my story. The moment I realized everything had to change."

### Scene Context
*Stacy sits on her bed, holding her phone. It's late. She's been scrolling through messages—old conversations, patterns she can't unsee.*

### Stacy's Voice
"I kept doing the same thing. Reaching out when I was lonely. Pulling back when things got real. I couldn't see it at the time, but I was stuck in a cycle I created."

*Flash of her scrolling, checking her phone obsessively.*

### Stacy's Voice (continues)
"Then I met Danny. And I thought *this* would be different. But I brought the same version of myself to him. The scared one. The one who couldn't stay vulnerable."

*Scene: Stacy and Danny together, laughing. Then Stacy alone, anxious, overthinking.*

### Stacy's Voice (vulnerable)
"I didn't know how to ask for what I needed. I didn't even know *what* I needed. So I did what I always did—I ran. Or I clung. Or I disappeared."

---

## Scene 2: The Turning Point

### Stacy's Voice
"The hardest part wasn't seeing what I was doing. It was understanding *why*. And that's where things started to shift."

*Montage of Stacy and Danny in different moments—conflict, connection, distance, hope.*

### Stacy's Voice (growing realization)
"I realized that understanding my patterns wasn't about fixing myself or 'winning' Danny back. It was about becoming the person I wanted to be. For me."

---

## Scene 3: The Invitation (Transition to "Put On My Shoes")

### Stacy's Voice
"Now I want to share this with you. Not because my story is yours. But because maybe you recognize yourself in it."

*The scene shifts. Stacy and Danny fade. The story becomes *your* story.*

### Narrator (Gently inviting)
"What if you could see your patterns the way Stacy saw hers? What if you could understand the 'why' behind what you do?"

### Stacy's Voice (warm, direct)
"Put on my shoes for a moment. Let's walk through this together."

---

## Scene 4: "Put On My Shoes" (Personalized)

### User-selected intro text appears:
"You're here because [anxiety/RSD/depression/attachment/ADHD/autism/addiction] is part of your life."

### Narrator continues
"And like Stacy, you might find yourself in patterns that don't serve you. Reaching out at the wrong times. Pulling away when connection matters. Saying yes when you meant no. Saying nothing when you wanted to scream."

### The Companion Unlocks
"But you don't have to figure this out alone. Meet your AI companion."

*Stacy (or Danny, or the user's chosen voice) appears*

### Stacy's Voice (as your companion)
"I've been where you are. I know what it feels like to not understand yourself. But I also know that understanding changes everything."
